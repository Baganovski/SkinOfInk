Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

Skin Of Ink. ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you contact us or visit this website, and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Details you give us directly — such as your name, email address and phone number — when you enquire or book an appointment.
  • Basic technical information your browser sends automatically, such as your device and browser type, used to keep the site working and secure.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and arrange and manage your appointments.

We do not sell your personal information, and we only share it where necessary to provide our service or where required by law.
